About Me

I am a 23 year old graduate of Computer Games Design at Solent University. I learned to program when I was 14 years old and started learning to make games on my own at 16. When I finished my A Levels in Computing and Business at 18, I decided to study Games Design at Solent University to learn from professionals who had worked in the industry. I have participated in dozens of Game Jams with a large variety of time scales and themes, working both solo and with teams. Some of my games have got relatively popular on itch. After graduating I started my current job as an IT Technician, and currently make games in my free time. My first Full release game, NodeRunner, released on Steam March 8th 2024
